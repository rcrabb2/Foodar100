How it Works

Register for an account

The first thing you need to do is register for an account. After registering, you will be able to submit requests for pickup and have your food delievered to your door.

Order your food

Place an order for food from a local restaurant and the system wil automatically find the nearest driver at the best cost

Confirm your request

Before any request is submitted, you have a chance to review it and make any changes order cancellations
